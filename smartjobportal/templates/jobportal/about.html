ATS Score Formula

ATS = (0.5 × Skill Match%)
    + (0.3 × BERT Semantic Similarity)
    + (0.2 × Keyword Density)

ATS ≥ 80  →  High   (70–90% shortlist chance)
ATS 60–79 →  Medium (40–70% shortlist chance)
ATS < 60  →  Low    (<40% shortlist chance)
